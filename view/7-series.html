The flowing silhouette of the BMW M760Li xDrive Sedan is in itself enough to promise the perfect symbiosis of the most sublime elegance and maximum performance. The undisputed top model of the 7 Series displays everything that can only be described with superlatives. Equipped with the powerful BMW M TwinPower Turbo 12-cylinder petrol engine, it attains the 100 km/h with its dedicated 585 hp in only 3.8 seconds. Together with the perfectly coordinated suspension components from the house of BMW M, this makes a surge of pure adrenaline simply inevitable. This limousine refines its racing genes with a ride comfort of the absolute luxury class.
